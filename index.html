Our Procedures

We’ve got everything covered for your needs

Medical Coding

If your practice needs coding help, we have certified coders available. We work with all specialties and can help maximize your revenue.

Medical Billing Services

We handle our clients’ medical billing and collecting procedures so they can focus on what they do best…provide quality care to their patients.

Charge Entry

You will provide us the demographic and charge info for each patient. Your assigned medical billers enter the charges daily.

Claims Submission

Our billing experts will make sure clean claims are submitted. If there are any rejections, after corrections re-submission is done in a timely manner.

Payment Posting

ERAs and EOBs are verified and posted in a timely manner. We verify to make sure appropriate payment is made on each claim.

Claims Correction & Denials Management

Our team will review each denial & take appropriate action. We will make corrections and resubmit the claims and file an appeal if deemed necessary.

Claims Appeal Services

Time constraints placed on appeals can make it difficult to redeem the money you deserve in a timely manner when an initial claim determination is made. An expert coder who is knowledgeable in the claims appeal process and stays current with the most up- to-date. Medicare and insurance procedure codes and modifiers is needed to assess these denials.

A/R Management & Cleanup

Our experts work your outstanding accounts receivable accounts to redeem missing revenue, including from write-offs. This can include denied insurance claims. We define trends and help you to improve collection abilities.

Credentialing and Contracting

MediPal Solution offers ongoing as well as initial enrollment. We will negotiate contracts on your behalf. Let our team take care of this painful process for you.
